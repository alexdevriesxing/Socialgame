# Sakura Crest v1.5 — Commercial Art & Walkable District

This release converts the bedroom and every off-campus destination from menu-only scenes into free-roaming 2D areas while replacing remaining placeholder-style presentation.

## Release gates

- Fourteen walkable maps: bedroom plus thirteen district locations.
- Unique collision geometry, valid spawn, camera containment and two exits per map.
- Wandering visiting classmates and location-specific interactions.
- Illustrated academy and district environments with commercial UI framing.
- Illustrated district map, gift shop, wardrobe and collections.
- No placeholder-card or placeholder-map validator flags.
- Offline, desktop and mobile browser QA with screenshot evidence.

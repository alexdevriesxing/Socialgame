# Sakura Crest Art Direction

## Visual identity

Crisp contemporary anime school-life presentation with warm cream paper tones, deep navy framing, sakura rose accents, clear sky blues and restrained gold highlights. Environments use readable top-down/three-quarter staging, material-specific surfaces, layered shadows and purposeful props.

## Quality rules

1. No empty placeholder rectangles, generic wireframes or temporary labels masquerading as art.
2. Every location needs a distinct silhouette, material palette, focal landmark and interaction staging.
3. Characters must be grounded with shadows and consistent scale across school and city maps.
4. UI must retain hierarchy at 960×540 and remain readable when scaled to mobile landscape.
5. Modal interfaces must suppress unrelated HUD and notification clutter.
6. Art changes must preserve collision and interaction coordinates or update their tests together.
7. Every release must include automated canvas-complexity checks and manual screenshot review.
